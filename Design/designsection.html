FInal Visual Aesthetic and Elements

When it came down to the final aesthetic of my website, I decided to go with minimalism. Not only do I think it looks cleaner, it also was a lot easier to implement. However this did mean that the elements that I want to show must be able to express the idea well. Meaning that a lot of thought was put into the colour palette aswell as the font choice. This also meant that I would not have to use as many icons as I would normally use.
